Net Solutions & Security is a Texas-based low-voltage security and technology company in Austin, TX. We offer a wide range of defense, surveillance, access, and automation products backed by professional design, installation, and monitoring. Specializing in serving local businesses, our many satisfied customers continue to help us grow.

Customer Service

Customization

Customers come first, that's why we provide free consultation and an onsite walkthrough. There isn't a one size fits all system when it comes to security solutions, every business is unique, and every job requires custom installation.

Community

Our Story

NSS was founded on providing proven results to our clients. Our team is passionate about the surveillance industry, combined we have more than 50 years' experience. Our reputation is built on caring for our customers and maintaining ongoing relationships. Too many times, other companies leave before a job is complete. We saw that things needed to change, and we provide services enabling ongoing support for our clients – from small boutique owners to corporate commercial buildings. Local technicians upgrade, repair, and maintain security systems to always keep you safe.

Our Process

Free Consultation

Contact us for an initial assessment of your property's needs. We will present a free estimate and consultation and discuss the best steps moving forward.

This includes a complete breakdown of services and parts, and helping you decide the optimal design for your business.

Walk-through

Our trained technicians will discover what you're looking for and schedule an onsite walk-through.

We analyze both the perimeter and interior to understand the most important placement of hardware and data installation.

Digital Blueprint

After your vision is finalized, we get to work crafting a digital map of the job.

We create a digital model of your business and show you exactly where installation will occur and how the technology will work. This includes crafting an IT plan for maintaining future success.

Affordable Rate

Once installation is finished, we provide service plans with affordable monthly rates that maintains your security system and data management.

Our local team provides warranty work and necessary upgrades. Enjoy a secure cloud storage system for accessing your data from anywhere in the world.

Sales

NSS security consultants provide a free, on-site survey before making any recommendations regarding products, services or pricing.

Net Solutions & Security gains a clear idea of each client’s specific needs, as well as an understanding of available solutions based on both customer need and site requirements.

Design

Our design consultants are trained to survey properties and provide a comprehensive proposal, including specifications, maps, hardware placement, and custom directives.

This helps us meet our clients’ needs within budget, improves installation efficiency, and reduces likelihood of unforeseen variables arising during the installation process.

Installation

All NSS employees must pass a State of Texas background check. Our installation technicians are insured, have workers’ compensation, and are licensed by the State of Texas.

Before beginning any project, our staff receive a thorough requirements brief from the sales and design team. This ensures that they have a clear understanding of site specifications and project tasks, and it gives us a benchmark for documentation throughout the lifestyle of the project.

Customer Safety

Upon project completion, clients receive training on new equipment from the installation technician. We make it a priority to support our customers on all systems for the life of the hardware.

We’ll be there if products don’t live up to their technical specs and warranties. Customer service is the cornerstone to the long-lasting relationships we work to build with each of our clients.
