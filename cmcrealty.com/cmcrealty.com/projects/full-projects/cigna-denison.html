CIGNA Denison

  • City: Denison, TX
  • Type: 140,000sf - Office Building
  • Services: Development, Const. Mgt., Project Leasing

CIGNA Denison is a 140,000 square foot call center facility located in Denison, Texas. CIGNA had been operating for many years in Sherman-Denison area in a collection of old facilities. CMC consolidated all of the requirements for CIGNA and built a 2 story, tilt wall building that was modern, efficient, yet cost-effective. The building has large floor plates and ample surface parking for the dense call center environment.
