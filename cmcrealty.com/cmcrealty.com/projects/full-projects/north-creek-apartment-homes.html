North Creek Apartment Homes

  • City: Hutto, TX
  • Type: 310 Units - Apartments
  • Services: Development, Const. Mgt., Project Leasing

North Creek is a 310 unit apartment project in the rapidly growing Austin suburb of Hutto. The project is surrounded by a 500 acre master planned community that will consist of over 700 single family homes, senior living and retail. North Creek consists of 13 buildings and will offer state of the art apartment amenities at an affordable price point. Residents will be able to walk to schools in the area and easily take the Toll 130 highway for the work commute. The projected is expected to be completed around March of 2019.
