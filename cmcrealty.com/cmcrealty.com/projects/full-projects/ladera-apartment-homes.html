Ladera Apartment Homes

  • City: Austin, TX
  • Project: 308 Units - Apartments
  • Building Type: 12 Building Garden - 3 Story and 3/4 Split buildings

Ladera Apartment Homes is a 308 unit luxury multifamily development located in Austin, Texas. This Class “A” Property consists of twelve buildings containg 269,073 square feet – built into a beautiful site featuring rolling hills and dozens of large, heritage Live Oak trees. Community amenities include a resort style pool, elegant clubhouse, state of the art business center, professional quality gym with a yoga room, a dog park, private garages, and superior landscaping. Residents also enjoy unit amenities such as granite counter tops, contemporary fixtures, custom trimming, designer appliances, nine foot ceilings, private patios, and spacious walk-in closets.

Ladera provides residents the opportunity to live in a luxurious and green setting in a neighborhood which provides easy access to major employment centers, shopping and recreational destinations, as well as an abundance of dining options.
