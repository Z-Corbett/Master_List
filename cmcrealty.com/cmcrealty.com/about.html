Our History 

CMC – Construction Management Corporation was founded in 1990, in Dallas, Texas to provide construction management services to third party clients. After several years as a successful business, CMC eventually evolved to become a full-service commercial real estate development company. At this time it was appropriate to change the name of the company and thus in 1995 the present day CMC – Commercial Realty Group was formed.

Together, the principals at CMC collectively bring over 50 years of real estate experience to the company. With their portfolio and reputation in the industry, CMC’s clients have entrusted them to develop over 14 million square feet of real estate across the United States including North Texas, San Antonio, Houston and Denver. Although CMC began as a developer of industrial buildings, over the years our expertise has diversified to other types of commercial real estate products including multifamily, retail, office and data centers. In over 20 years of existence, CMC has grown into a strong and competitive turnkey real estate developer with a broad experience in every aspect of the real estate business.
