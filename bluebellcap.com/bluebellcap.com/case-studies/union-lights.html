Union Lights

NANTUCKET, MA

Getting away from the city is what makes life on island so enviable. Bringing a touch of downtown sophistication to a foursome of row houses just minutes from the wharf represented a rare opportunity. By investing in a vision for chic city living on Union Street, we co-authored a new chapter for a valuable quartet of overlooked, but highly-prized centrally-located summer homes.
