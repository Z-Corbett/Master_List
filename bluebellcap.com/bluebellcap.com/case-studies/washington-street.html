Washington Street

NANTUCKET, MA

Nothing is more idyllic than watching the boats come into Nantucket Harbor. Tucked off Washington Street as you head out of Town, a patch of land facing out towards the Atlantic presented Blue Bell Capital with an unmissable opportunity. This quartet of nestled paddleboard-ready bungalows, each facing out towards the water is underway, with delivery expected in 2019.
