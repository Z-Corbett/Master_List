Cannonbury Lane

SIASCONSET, MA

Summer on Nantucket should be simple. Giving islander-goers a chance to recapture their youth by way of a fully dune-scaped luxury beach community made Cannonbury Lane unique and an unignorable opportunity. Our second luxury island community endeavor on island proves that Nantucket's record-breaking growth as an ever-booming island enclave isn't limited to Town. Expected delivery: 2019 / 2020.

DETAILS

30+ Acres, Low Beach Beach & 400 Acre Nature Preserve Proximity, Holistic Streetscaping, Greenhouse, Yoga Deck, Gym, Cutting Gardens and Walking Paths
