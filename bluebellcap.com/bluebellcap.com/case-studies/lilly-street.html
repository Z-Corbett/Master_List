Lilly Street

NANTUCKET, MA

Walking out of the backdoor and right into a park comes at a premium anywhere. Private park access and walkability into Town on Nantucket makes Lilly Street all the more special. When finished, this duo of Norwegian-inspired hideaways will offer islanders something extremely hard to come by on island: space to breathe. Expected delivery: 2019.
